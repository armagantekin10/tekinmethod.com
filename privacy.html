Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

Who we are

Tekin Method is an independent consulting service operated by Armagan Tekin, based in Istanbul, Turkey. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use tekinmethod.com and our related services.

What data we collect

When you use our free diagnostic tools (hosted on ChatGPT), we do not collect or store your responses. Those interactions are governed by OpenAI's privacy policy.

When you purchase a Pattern Report or Professional Diagnostic, we collect: your name, email address, company name, company size, and payment information. Payment processing is handled by iyzico. We do not store credit card numbers on our servers.

When you engage in a Professional Diagnostic that involves Salesforce data analysis, we access your Salesforce organization data only with your explicit written authorization, only for the scope and duration of the engagement, and only in read-only mode.

How we use your data

We use your information to deliver the service you purchased, to send your report and related communications, to process your payment, and to improve our diagnostic methodology using anonymized, aggregated patterns. We do not sell, share, or rent your personal data to third parties.

Payment processing

Payments are processed securely through iyzico (iyzico Odeme Hizmetleri A.S.), a licensed payment institution regulated by the Banking Regulation and Supervision Agency of Turkey (BDDK). iyzico processes your payment data in accordance with PCI DSS standards. For bank transfer payments, standard banking data protection applies.

Data retention

We retain your contact and engagement data for 24 months after service delivery. Salesforce access credentials are revoked immediately upon report delivery. Aggregated, anonymized diagnostic data may be retained indefinitely for methodology improvement.

Your rights

Under the Turkish Personal Data Protection Law (KVKK, Law No. 6698) and, where applicable, the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at [email protected].

Cookies

tekinmethod.com does not use cookies or tracking scripts. We do not run analytics on the website.
